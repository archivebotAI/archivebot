Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

ArchiveBot is built for simple local use. You can chat without creating an account.

Accounts: If you create an account, the server stores your name, email, and a protected password hash in a local data file.

Chat: Messages are sent to the local backend, then forwarded to the external AI endpoint so ArchiveBot can reply. Chat history is kept in your browser while the page is open.

Sessions: Sign-in uses a local session cookie so the app remembers you on this browser.

Your choice: You can use the app in guest mode, sign out anytime, and avoid entering personal details beyond account creation.
